Our Story

Dee

My first memory of Elisia is about 5 years ago. We met a year before we actually started dating by attending an event for an ex's friend. We sat in the same row and she offered me a Starburst. It was a new pack and I didnt know her well so felt bad asking for a red one as thats all I eat. I ended up still asking and because of that the ex was mad and we went home. Fast forward to three years ago, I'm single and out to sushi with Shelby and just discussing life. My track record wasn't the best so we decided I need an actual lesbian girlfriend who knows what she wants and if I possibly knew anyone. I thought of Elisia immediately which was weird due to the fact we only met that once. Shelby and I used our snooping skills and was able to find her on Facebook. I decided the Saki liquid courage gave me enough confidence to reach out and send her a message. I pushed send and a few minutes later I had a new message! We ended up talking pretty much the whole next day and decided to meet up soon after. A few months later we were official and since then we have been trying to travel the world. We have visited Puerto Rico, Cancun, Rome, Barcelona, Dominican Republic, Puerto Vallarta, Australia, and even got engaged in Bali. There are still so many places we would love to see. Even after three years together, I’m still amazed by how passionate, caring, easy going, and goofy she is, and I’m so excited to spend the rest of our lives growing together.
